CAMEL

A camel is a large animal that lives in the desert. It has one or two humps on its back that store fat, which helps it survive when there is no food or water. Camels can walk long distances in the hot sun and go for many days without drinking. They have long legs, thick lips, and wide feet to move easily on sand.

DEER

A deer is a gentle wild animal with slender legs and antlers (horns) on the male’s head. It lives in forests and grasslands and eats grass, leaves, and fruits. Deer are known for being fast runners and having good hearing and smell to escape predators.

DOG

A dog is a domestic animal and a loyal pet. It is known as man’s best friend. Dogs help people in many ways—guarding homes, herding animals, and assisting police. They are intelligent, friendly, and can be trained to follow commands.

EAGLE

An eagle is a large, powerful bird known for its sharp eyesight and strong wings. It flies high in the sky and hunts fish and small animals. Eagles build large nests on cliffs or tall trees and are symbols of strength and freedom.

GRAFFE

A giraffe is the tallest land animal with a long neck and long legs. It lives in African grasslands and eats leaves from tall trees, especially acacia trees. Giraffes have spotted coats and a gentle nature.

PANDA

A panda is a black and white bear that lives in the mountains of China. It mainly eats bamboo and is known for its cute and gentle nature. Pandas spend most of their time eating and resting and are a symbol of peace and friendship.
